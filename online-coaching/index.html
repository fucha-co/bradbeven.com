Online Coaching

Brad Beven

Brad Beven Triathlon provides personally tailored, on-line training programs. Programs are tailored to your specific goals, background and capacity/ commitments, and provide detailed descriptions of sessions and photos/ videos of specific activities (e.g. stretching).

These are popular with people wanting more structure to their training – and ensure you have greater direction in the work-outs that you do. You can combine them with squad sessions (either with BBT or other groups).

With 30 years experience in the triathlon world, Brad can certainly help to get you where you want to go.
